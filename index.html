Frequently Asked Questions

When should I arrive in Jamaica?

We recommend arriving at least one day before the wedding (July 11th) to settle in and enjoy the resort. This gives you time to relax, adjust to the beautiful Caribbean atmosphere, and join us for any pre-wedding festivities!

What is the weather like in July?

July in Montego Bay is warm and tropical with temperatures averaging 80-90°F (27-32°C). Pack light, breathable clothing, sunscreen, and don't forget your swimwear! There may be occasional afternoon showers, but they're usually brief.

Is this an adults-only celebration?

While we love your little ones, we've decided to make this an adults-only celebration to allow all our guests to relax and enjoy the festivities. We hope this gives parents a chance for a well-deserved vacation!

How do I book my stay at Sandals Royal Caribbean?

Contact Sandals directly and mention our wedding to receive special group rates! You can also reach out to us for our travel coordinator's contact information who can help arrange your accommodations and any special requests.

What activities are available at the resort?

Sandals Royal Caribbean offers unlimited water sports, scuba diving, golf, spa services, multiple gourmet restaurants, and access to their private island. All meals, premium drinks, and most activities are included in your stay!

Do I need a passport to travel to Jamaica?

Yes! A valid passport is required for US citizens traveling to Jamaica. Make sure your passport is valid for at least 6 months beyond your travel dates. Check with your country's travel requirements well in advance.

Will transportation be provided from the airport?

Sandals provides complimentary round-trip airport transfers from Sangster International Airport (MBJ) in Montego Bay. The resort is approximately 15 minutes from the airport. Details will be provided after you book your stay.

What about gifts?

Your presence is the greatest gift we could ask for! If you'd like to contribute to our future together, we'll have information about our honeymoon fund. No physical gifts please, as we'll be traveling light!
